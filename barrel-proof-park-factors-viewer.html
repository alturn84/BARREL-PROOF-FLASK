How The Index Works
The Barrel Proof Park Factor (BP Index) combines two layers: static park DNA and dynamic weather conditions.

Park DNA is sourced from Baseball Savant Statcast Park Factors (3-year rolling 2022–2024) and cross-referenced with FanGraphs Park Factors. All indices are on a 100 = MLB average scale. A park with a Runs index of 108 produces 8% more runs than average. A park with an HR index of 90 produces 10% fewer home runs.

Weather Modifiers are additive adjustments applied on top of park DNA each day. Wind direction and speed is the dominant variable. Temperature, humidity, and roof status are secondary factors.

Composite Score is a weighted blend of the three adjusted indices, shifted so that 0 = neutral. Positive = hitter-friendly, negative = pitcher-friendly.
BP Index = (adjRuns × 0.40) + (adjHR × 0.35) + (adjXBH × 0.25) − 100
