## Score Recap

```
[Away Team] [Away Score], [Home Team] [Home Score]
[One key stat or moment].
```

Example:
```
Yankees 5, Red Sox 3
Cole: 6 IP, 2 ER, 9 K. New York takes two of three at Fenway.
```

---

## Pitcher Performance Highlight

```
[Player name]: [IP] IP, [ER] ER, [K] K[, notable detail if warranted]
[1-sentence context — team record, streak, standings].
```

Example:
```
Skubal: 8 IP, 0 ER, 11 K
Detroit wins its fourth straight. Skubal's ERA drops to 1.84.
```

---

## Notes on Templates

These are starting points, not rigid scripts. Allan may adjust phrasing before posting. The templates exist to ensure posts lead with facts and maintain consistent structure.

All variable fields in brackets must be filled with actual data before the draft is staged for approval.
